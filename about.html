    Vision & Mission

    Our mission is to open eyes to different cultures. We use film to stir the debate on world communication, sustainability and the fight against culture ignorance. We believe that film has the unprecedented capacity to raise awareness and provide new insight. What cannot be accomplished by spoken and written language, is sometimes achieved by motion picture.

    Our vision is that film as an art form is an exceptionally strong medium. Where words and writing sometimes fail to really touch people, cinema can break through indifference or cynicism.
